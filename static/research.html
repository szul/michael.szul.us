Research

Online Education/Psychology Research

  • June 2011 - August 2013

    Reviewed literature and data related to online learning, learning management systems (LMS), computer literacy, personality types, and grade performance. Performed statistical analysis on data sets. Tested computer literacy and personality measures. Responsible for writing the rough draft of academic papers based on the research.
