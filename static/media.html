  • Nights with Alice Cooper. October 2004.

    My very early years on the Internet were spent making posts about the paranormal before the term "blog" was even coined. I used to help out Norman Williams of New Jersey Paranormal Investigations (NJPI) with various cases. Those cases combined with other paranormal experiences inspired me to create a web site (originally called The Midknight Club) that eventually morphed into The Mad Ghoul (or madghoul.com). The switch to madghoul.com and a more personal narrative was partly inspired by the television show Freakylinks.
